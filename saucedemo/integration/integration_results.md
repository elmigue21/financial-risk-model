## Notes
- The `/v1/` login URL permanently redirects to the current SauceDemo site; tests use it as the entry point and target the served DOM.
- **Chrome intermittency is a known environment/SPA interaction, not a product defect in the flow:** SauceDemo’s cart/checkout links are href-less `onClick` anchors, and Chrome occasionally dispatches a click before the React handler is ready (this also occurs at baseline, without screenshots). Firefox and Edge are reliable.
- Opera is Chromium-based and supported by the driver factory but is not installed on this machine, so those runs are reported as **blocked** (pytest SKIPPED).
- Screenshots per step are captured under `integration/screenshots/<test>/<browser>/`.
